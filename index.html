(A) / About

UkrDim is the site of a company that builds turnkey houses throughout Ukraine. Their main service is to build a modern and beautiful house so that customers do not waste their nerves on it. Their motto: "Build simply!".

The design of the site itself is a little outdated, so it was decided to develop a redesign for greater customer trust.

(B) / Overview

problem

The design of the site is outdated, it looks a bit overloaded. Some color combinations cause strain on the eyes and make the text difficult to read.

The navigation system is not always obvious and sometimes confusing. It is difficult to find the sections or pages you need, which makes it difficult to use the site. There is no adaptation.

solution

The design has been modernized, the brand color has been used, accents have been placed, and the readability of the text has been made easier. Navigation on the site has been improved and made more intuitive.

All main pages are placed in the header, and on the main page of the site the user is introduced to the product, benefits, everything is structured and aimed at increasing conversion. Convenient adaptation has been developed.
